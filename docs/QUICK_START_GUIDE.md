## ğŸƒ 5-Minute Quick Start

### Step 1: Create Your First Canvas

1. Look at the **center** of the screen - you'll see "Create Your Canvas"
2. Click **"Generate with AI"** to start with AI image generation
3. Or click **"Import File"** to upload an existing image (up to 7 files!)

### Step 2: Generate an Image

1. In the canvas, type a prompt like: *"A magical forest with glowing mushrooms"*
2. Click **Generate** or press **Enter**
3. Wait 10-30 seconds for your AI image to appear

### Step 3: Edit Your Image

1. Select the **AI Inpainting** tool (âœï¸ icon in top toolbar)
2. Draw a mask over the area you want to change
3. Type what you want in that area
4. Click **Apply** to see the magic!

### Step 4: Try the Timeline

1. Click **"Timeline Mode"** button below the canvas
2. You'll see 3 tracks: **Visual**, **Audio**, **FX**
3. Click the **+** button on any track to add a test clip
4. Drag clips to reposition them
5. Use **Play** button to preview

## ğŸ¬ Timeline Mode

The Timeline lets you arrange media on multiple tracks:

### Tracks

- **Visual** (Blue) - Images, videos
- **Audio** (Green) - Sound effects, music
- **FX** (Purple) - Text overlays, effects

### Basic Operations

1. **Add Clip**: Click + on a track, or drag from Recent Assets
2. **Move Clip**: Click and drag horizontally
3. **Resize Clip**: Drag the edges
4. **Delete Clip**: Select and click the X

### Cut & Mend

1. Drag on the time ruler to select a range
2. Click **"Cut Inside"** to remove that section
3. The timeline automatically "mends" - closing the gap!
